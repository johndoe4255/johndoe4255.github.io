How to place an order

Step one
Choose one or more products (note the minimum orders) then send an email to sale@danishbitweed.trade specifying exactly what types of products you wish, and where to ship them.

All products are sent in personalized letters in a very discreet way. You wont know whats inside from looking at the exterior.

 Step two
Wait for the confirmation email containing payment address and order specifics. If you can verify that the order is correct and what you wish for, then simply transfer the specified amount of bitcoin to the payment address and your order is completed.

If, for any reason, your order cannot be completed then the bitcoins are returned to the address they was sent from. No products are shipped prior to receiving payment and no refunds will be given for products which are shipped succesfully.

Step three
Wait for your product(s) to arrive! I cannot guarantee any specific time-frame but usually it takes no longer than one week.

Hashish

Hashish is often more potent than other types of marijuana products, a good hashish is made only from the resin of the flowers(trichomes).

Hashish is also a more 'heavy' high compared to the light buzz of most pot, even so, it is the preferred marijuana product among the locals of Christiania.

Types

Kashmir - light hashish, sweet and most popular by far.

Marokh - lightdark hashish, often a more greasy texture and heavy smoke

ICE - Concentrated, pure THC residues, VERY potent (min. order 0.35 )

Marijuana seeds

Marijuana seeds are some of the easiest to care for, especially those from Denmark or other tempered climates.
Because of the poor weather conditions, the plants have been bred to withstand even the most bothersome weathers while enjoying the benefits of sunlight to the fullest when the chance is there.
The seeds can grow both indoor and outdoor without issues, although a proper grow-setup would make them thrive, they can do just fine sitting in your windows, or in a garden.

be aware that i cannot guarantee the ratios of female/male seeds upfront, nor will i guarantee the specific amount of seeds you receive from an order, unless you ask for a specific amount in your order, in which case an accurate price will be determined.
There are only one strain of seeds available.

Pot/Skunk

Pot or Skunk, the names are plentiful and the differences can be hard to spot. Pot is in most cases a lot weaker than skunk is, with THC levels of skunk often tripling that of pot.
In many parts of the world, including the United States and Central America, pot is the most common cannabis product consumed among the general population.

It is also the most common way for medical marijuana patients to medicate themselves, often using a vaporizer, or pipe to smoke from. Pot and skunk can be smoked without mixing it with tobacco, unlike all types of hashish, which makes it ideal for cancer patients etc. where tobacco is best to avoid.

i can not guarantee the exact strain of skunk or pot that you receive before i have acquired the order due to rapid changes in supply.
If specified in your order, i will inform you of strain when i let you know that your order has been sent.
Minimum order is 0.05
